Worldometer is run by an international team of developers, researchers, and volunteers with the goal of making world statistics available in a thought-provoking and time relevant format to a wide audience around the world. It is published by a small and independent digital media company based in the United States. Click image for more information.



USAFacts presents metrics on all US governments, from federal records to a growing collection of state and county data, including coronavirus data. exclusively use publicly available government data presenting a vast array of reports on US spending, revenue, population and demographics, and policy outcomes.
