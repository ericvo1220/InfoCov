General Information About Covid

Covid 19 is caused by the pathogen SARS-Cov-2 that affects the respiratory system. It was first found in Wuhan, China in late 2019 which brought pandemic all over the world afterward. Most of the cases do not require special treatment and recover with mild to moderate respiratory discomfort. For some cases especially for older people and underlying health conditions, the condition can worsen and requires treatment.

Prevention

The main way to prevent Covid-19 is to be aware of it. Some of the ways that will help to prevent this disease are as follow-

    -Wear a mask in public
    -Maintain 6 ft distance with another person
    -Wash hands frequently with soap or use sanitizer
    -Get Vaccinated
